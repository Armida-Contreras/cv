About Me

I am Armida Contreras, a passionate and skilled web designer and developer with a strong eye for detail and a knack for creating visually appealing and user-friendly websites. I have a deep understanding of front-end web development technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as experience with popular frameworks and libraries like React and Bootstrap.

I also have a background in Embedded System Engineering. With my strong technical skills and creative mindset, I have successfully developed and designed a cash payment kiosk with a monitoring system. This project involved designing the user interface, implementing payment functionalities, and integrating hardware components for seamless operation.

Throughout my career, I have worked on various projects, collaborating with clients to understand their requirements and deliver high-quality websites that align with their vision. I am constantly exploring new trends and techniques in web design to stay updated and enhance my skills.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new breakthrough innovation with internet of things, experimenting with photography, or immersing myself in a good book. I believe in continuous learning and personal growth, and I am always excited to take on new challenges and expand my knowledge in the ever-evolving field of technology.
